Visakhapatnam

Kailasagiri

Kailasagiri is a hilltop park in the city of Visakhapatnam in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. The park was developed by the Visakhapatnam Metropolitan Region Development Authority and comprises 380 acres of land covered with flora and tropical trees.
    

INS Kursura

INS Kursura (S20) was a Kalvari-class diesel-electric submarine of the Indian Navy. She was India's fifth submarine. Kursura was commissioned on 18 December 1969 and was decommissioned on 27 February 2001 after 31 years of service. It remains as a tourist attraction for all science enthusiasts.
    

Indira Gandhi Zoological Park

Indira Gandhi Zoological Park is located amidst Kambalakonda Reserve Forest in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India. It is the third largest zoo in the country. A fun place for kids and adults alike!

    

R K Beach

RK Beach also known as Ramakrishna Beach is situated on the East coast of Bay of Bengal in Visakhapatnam. It is located near Dolphin's Nose. RK beach is a beach for to spend some relaxed time, enjoy strolls across the beach, sunbath and more. A fun place for adults and kids.
